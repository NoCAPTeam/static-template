WHAT ARE GHGs?

Greenhouse gases (GHGs) are gases that trap heat in earth's atmosphere and are released primarily by burning fossil fuels.

This results in:

  • Rising Sea Levels
  • Higher Temperatures
  • Extreme Weather
  • Accelerated Climate Change
